Body Text - Roboto & Arial (Sans-serif)
Located in Mawaque, Mabalacat City, Pampanga, Garage Café is your friendly neighborhood coffee shop offering quality drinks and comfort food in a welcoming atmosphere.

Used for: Paragraphs, descriptions, card content, all body text

Navigation - Lucida Sans (Condensed)

Used for: Navigation menu items only

Color Combinations

Dark on Light

Hero sections, headers, and footer areas use dark brown backgrounds with off-white text for maximum contrast and readability.

Light Backgrounds

Main content areas use cream backgrounds with dark brown text, providing a warm, comfortable reading experience.

Gradient Accents

Cards and feature sections use subtle gradients from lighter brown to cream for visual interest and depth.

Implementation Notes

  • Warm & Comfort: The brown palette creates an inviting, cozy café atmosphere
  • Readability: High contrast between dark browns and light creams ensures text is easy to read
  • Modern Functional: Clean design with warm colors balances traditional café feel with modern web aesthetics
  • Brand Consistency: All colors derived from approved style guide for consistent brand identity
  • Accessibility: Color combinations meet WCAG contrast requirements for web accessibility
