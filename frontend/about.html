ABOUT GARAGE CAFÉ

Your neighborhood coffee shop in Mabalacat City

Our Journey

Our Story

Garage Café is located in Mawaque, Mabalacat City, Pampanga—a developing community with a growing number of local businesses and an increasing demand for quality hangout spots.

Built with passion to serve good drinks and comfort food, we've become a welcoming destination for students, employees, families, and coffee lovers. Over time, we've expanded our services to include meal pre-orders and event coffee setups for birthdays, meetings, and small gatherings.

More than just a café, we're transforming into a workspace and study hub where you can relax, work, and connect with others in the community.

What We Offer

Services & Offerings

Affordable Coffee & Food

Quality drinks and comfort food at budget-friendly prices. We believe good coffee shouldn't break the bank, making us perfect for students and professionals.

Study & Work Space

A quiet, comfortable environment with Wi-Fi and power outlets. Whether you're studying, working remotely, or catching up on projects, we've got you covered.

Meal Pre-Orders

Busy schedule? Pre-order your meals and pick them up at your convenience. Perfect for those on-the-go moments when you need a quick, delicious meal.

Event Coffee Setups

Planning a birthday, meeting, or small gathering? We provide event coffee setups to make your occasion special with our quality beverages.

Our Vision

Building Community

We envision Garage Café as more than just an ordinary food establishment. Our goal is to be a true community hub—a place where neighbors meet, students study, professionals work, and everyone feels welcome.

Located conveniently near Metro Clark Homes in Sapang Biabas, we're proud to serve the Mawaque community and contribute to the growing local business scene in Mabalacat City.
